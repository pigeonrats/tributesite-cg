Nene Thomas

Fantasy Painter and Artist


Nene Thomas is a professional painter and digital artist, beginning her career in 1994 creating art for Magic the Gathering. She is known for painting whimsical fairies and fantasy art. She currently resides in Oklahoma still creating art of various mediums and working on her series, Zarryiostrom.
